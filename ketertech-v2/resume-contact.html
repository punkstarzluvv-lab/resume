PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

I’m a multidisciplinary creative operator with experience across creative direction, fashion branding, graphic design, web design, AI workflows, sales, real estate lead generation, and business development. My background was built through real execution from an early age — launching my own clothing brand, designing apparel graphics and print-on-demand pieces, building websites, developing visual identities, growing social pages, and helping shape the presentation of brands, communities, and digital projects.

What makes my path different is that it was never limited to just design. Alongside my creative work, I developed a strong entrepreneurial foundation through sales-oriented environments and real estate-related work, where I gained experience with lead generation, data gathering, CRM organization, cold calling awareness, prospecting, follow-up logic, and understanding how business actually moves. That side of my experience taught me to think beyond aesthetics alone and to see the deeper infrastructure behind growth: trust, positioning, communication, systems, and conversion.

Over time, I’ve continued building across multiple lanes — fashion, branding, websites, content, social media, outreach, AI-assisted workflows, and digital business strategy. I understand how to translate ideas into brands, brands into visuals, and visuals into digital experiences that feel intentional, culturally aware, and commercially useful. I’m especially strong where presentation and utility meet: creating work that not only looks elevated, but also helps a person, brand, or business communicate better, earn more trust, and move more effectively in the market.

I also bring a level of resilience and commitment that shapes how I work. I’ve built through instability, sacrifice, pressure, and uncertainty, which sharpened my discipline, adaptability, and willingness to keep going when things are difficult. That experience made me more serious, more resourceful, and more driven to create work that has substance. I approach projects with creative vision, business awareness, and a strong work ethic, with the goal of building brands, systems, and digital experiences that actually matter.

ABOUT ME

My background blends creativity, entrepreneurship, and digital execution. I started young by building my own clothing brand, designing graphics, creating products, and developing websites while learning how branding, image, and perception influence the way people respond to what they see. From there, my work expanded into broader digital and business-focused areas including social media content, website creation, creator support, community-facing media, sales thinking, AI-assisted workflows, and lead-generation strategy.

Real estate was a major part of shaping the way I think about business. It pushed me closer to the realities of lead flow, data, prospecting, CRM systems, cold calling, follow-up, and opportunity. That experience trained me to understand that good presentation alone is never enough. Behind every strong business is a structure that helps it move — clear communication, organized systems, trust signals, strong positioning, and a path that makes it easier for people to take action. That way of thinking continues to shape how I approach creative and digital work today.

At the same time, I’ve always been deeply creative. Fashion, music, branding, visuals, and identity have all played a major role in how I see the world and in the kind of work I’m drawn to build. I naturally gravitate toward projects where design and meaning come together — where something can feel visually strong, emotionally resonant, and strategically useful at the same time. That combination is what defines the way I work now: creative enough to shape a strong identity, and practical enough to build something that supports real business value.
